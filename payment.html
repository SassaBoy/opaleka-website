Opaleka Payment Terms

Effective Date: February 2025 | Secure & Transparent Transactions

Accepted Payment Methods

Opaleka supports secure transactions using the following payment methods:

  • Credit & Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard)
  • Mobile Money (E-wallets, Mobile Banking)
  • Bank Transfers & Electronic Fund Transfers (EFT)
  • Secure Payment Gateway (DPO Group)

Security Notice: All payment transactions are encrypted and processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways to ensure maximum security of your financial information.

Subscription & Billing

Service providers must subscribe to access Opaleka’s platform. The current subscription plan is:

  • 30-Day Subscription: NAD 200 (one-time payment)

Renewal Process: Subscriptions do not auto-renew. Service providers must manually renew their subscription at the end of each period to continue accessing the platform.

Financial Terms for Service Providers

All service providers are responsible for handling transactions directly with clients. Payments between clients and providers occur outside of the Opaleka platform.

Subscription Payments

  • Payments must be made before the subscription expiry date.
  • Accepted methods: EFT, Credit/Debit Cards, Mobile Wallets, Coupons.

Payment Process

Funds must be deposited into Opaleka’s designated account:

  • Bank Transfers (EFT): First National Bank - Opaleka Technologies (Pty) Ltd
  • Mobile Wallets: E-wallet, BlueWallet, EasyWallet, NedbankMoney
  • Payment Gateway: Secure transactions via DPO Group

Financial Terms

Clients make payments directly to service providers for services rendered. Opaleka does not process or handle these payments but provides a secure platform for service interactions.

Subscription for Service Providers

  • 30-Day Subscription: NAD 200 (one-time payment)

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard) via DPO Group
  • Bank Transfers (EFT) to Opaleka’s account
  • Mobile Wallets

Important Notice: Opaleka does not facilitate payments between clients and service providers. Clients must coordinate payments directly with their chosen service provider.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Our refund policy ensures fair treatment for all parties:

  • Subscription Cancellation: No refund for unused portions of a billing period.
  • Service Cancellation: Full refund if canceled within 24 hours of booking.
  • Dispute Resolution: Refund claims must be submitted within 7 days of the transaction.
  • Processing Time: Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days.

Payment Security & Compliance

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all transactions
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Compliance with PCI-DSS standards
  • Multi-factor authentication for high-value transactions
  • Real-time fraud detection and prevention

Security Advisory: Never share your payment credentials or verification codes. Opaleka staff will never ask for your complete card details or banking passwords.

Prohibited Activities

  • Engaging in fraudulent transactions or chargebacks
  • Using unauthorized or stolen payment methods
  • Circumventing Opaleka’s payment system
  • Sharing login credentials or financial data with third parties

Violations may result in account suspension or legal action.

Dispute Resolution & Legal Compliance

Governing Law

These payment terms are governed by the laws of Namibia.

Arbitration Agreement

  • Disputes must first go through informal negotiation.
  • If unresolved, parties may opt for binding arbitration through NAMFISA.
  • Legal action will be subject to Namibian courts if arbitration is not pursued.
