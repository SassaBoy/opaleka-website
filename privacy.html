Veldt Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2025 | Your Data, Your Control

Data Collection & Usage

Veldt collects and processes certain information to provide a seamless and secure service experience. Below is an overview of the data we collect and how it is used:

1. Information You Provide

When using our platform, you may be required to provide the following details:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and profile details.
  • Service Provider Information: Business name, address, service offerings, years of experience, and operating hours.
  • Client Booking Information: Details of bookings made, service requests, and appointment history.
  • Payment Details: Transaction records for services booked through our platform (Veldt does not store credit/debit card details).

2. Information Collected Automatically

We may collect certain technical and usage data when you interact with our platform, including:

  • Device Information: Your device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Interaction with our app, page visits, and feature engagement.
  • Location Data: If you grant permission, we may collect your location to enhance service recommendations.

3. How We Use Your Information

Veldt utilizes collected data to:

  • Enable and improve the booking process for clients and service providers.
  • Facilitate secure transactions and prevent fraud.
  • Enhance platform performance and user experience.
  • Communicate important updates, promotions, or service notifications.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill our obligations and legal requirements:

  • Active Accounts: Data is stored while your account remains active.
  • Inactive Accounts: If you stop using Veldt, your data is retained for one year before being anonymized.
  • Transaction Data: Payment records are stored for five years in compliance with financial regulations.
  • User Reviews & Ratings: These remain visible to maintain service provider credibility.

Transparency Notice: Veldt values privacy. Your data is never sold, and we use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

How We Protect Your Data

Veldt is committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your information. We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

Security Measure Description
End-to-End Encryption All sensitive data, including payment and personal details, are encrypted both in transit and at rest.
Regular Security Audits Our systems undergo periodic security checks to identify and mitigate potential vulnerabilities.
Strict Access Controls Only authorized personnel have access to specific data, ensuring confidentiality and minimizing risks.
Compliance with Privacy Regulations Veldt follows international data protection standards, including GDPR and local Namibian regulations.
Secure Data Centers We store data in highly secure, redundant servers to prevent data loss and ensure uninterrupted services.

Transparency Commitment: We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes. Our security practices are continuously updated to align with the latest cybersecurity standards.
