About Opaleka

Opaleka is more than just an app—it’s the start of something bigger. The name **Opaleka**, which means “to clean” in Oshiwambo, originally began as an idea for a **cleaning services platform**. But it quickly evolved into something much greater: a way to clean up **the disconnect between service providers and clients in Namibia**, creating opportunities, jobs, and a better future for all.

With Opaleka, anyone can **find and book trusted professionals**, and service providers can **grow their businesses** by connecting with clients effortlessly. This is just the beginning—the long-term vision is to expand Opaleka into a group of companies that drive innovation, employment, and transformation in Namibia.

Our Story

Opaleka started from a casual conversation that turned into something much bigger. In 2023, founder Deon Gewers was talking to a friend about opening a cleaning services company. That friend, whose name cannot be disclosed, had a simple but game-changing suggestion: "Why not just build an app instead?" The idea was to make it easier for people to book cleaning services through a mobile platform.

But then Deon had a realization—why stop at just cleaning services? Namibia needed something bigger. What if there was a platform where anyone could find and book **any** service provider, whether it’s an electrician, a mechanic, a tutor, or a photographer? That’s when Opaleka evolved beyond just cleaning services. The name took on a new meaning: not about sweeping floors, but about **cleaning up the system**—creating job opportunities, improving service accessibility, and making Namibia a better place through technology.

The vision doesn’t stop with the app. Opaleka is just the beginning. The long-term goal is to build the Opaleka Group of Companies—a network of businesses, all working towards one mission: **transforming Namibia**. From tech solutions to job creation, Opaleka is here to open doors, connect people, and shape the future.

We’re not just another app. **We’re a movement.** And this is only the start.

Our Mission

To **transform service delivery in Namibia** by creating a **seamless digital platform** where skilled professionals and clients connect effortlessly. Opaleka is not just a marketplace—it’s a movement toward **economic empowerment, technological growth, and accessible opportunities** for all Namibians.

Our Vision

To make **Opaleka the leading service platform in Namibia**, setting a new benchmark for **quality, reliability, and accessibility** in professional services. Our goal is to drive **digital transformation across all industries**, ensuring every Namibian has access to the services they need, when they need them.

Our Values

At the heart of Opaleka are **four core values**:

  • Trust: We build a **secure and reliable** platform where clients and service providers can engage with confidence.
  • Excellence: We uphold the **highest standards** in service quality, ensuring users get the best experience.
  • Innovation: We embrace **cutting-edge technology** to create smarter, more efficient solutions.
  • Community: We are driven by a mission to **uplift and empower** Namibians through economic opportunities.

The Future: Opaleka Group of Companies

Opaleka started as a **service booking app**, but it was never meant to stop there. This is just the beginning.

Our long-term vision is to build the **Opaleka Group of Companies**, a powerhouse of businesses dedicated to **technology, innovation, and job creation** in Namibia. Each company within the group will tackle different challenges—from **digital solutions to job creation and economic development**—but all will share a single purpose: to **empower Namibians and transform industries**.

We are not just **building an app**; we are **building a future** where every Namibian has access to better services, more opportunities, and a stronger economy.
