About Veldt

Veldt is more than just an app—it’s the beginning of a movement. The name **Veldt**, derived from the Afrikaans and Dutch word meaning “open grassland” or “field,” symbolizes vast opportunities and growth. But the journey to Veldt started with a different name—**Opaleka**, meaning “to make clean” in Oshiwambo. Originally envisioned as a **cleaning services platform**, the idea quickly expanded beyond cleaning to **bridging the gap between service providers and clients in Namibia**.

With Veldt, anyone can **find and book trusted professionals**, while service providers can **grow their businesses** with ease. What started as a simple idea to connect cleaners with clients has evolved into a mission to **create opportunities, drive innovation, and transform industries across Namibia**. And this is just the beginning—the long-term vision is to build Veldt into a network of companies that uplift communities and redefine service accessibility.

Our Story

It all started with a simple phone conversation in 2023. A discussion about launching a cleaning services company quickly turned into something much bigger. One idea sparked a game-changing suggestion: "Why not just build an app instead?" And just like that, **Opaleka**—which means "to make clean" in Oshiwambo—was born. The goal was to make it easier for people to find and book professional cleaning services effortlessly.

But as the idea took shape, a realization emerged—why stop at just cleaning? What if there was a platform where anyone in Namibia could find and book **any** service provider, from electricians and mechanics to tutors and photographers? This shift in perspective led to the evolution of Opaleka into **Veldt**.

The name Veldt, derived from the Afrikaans and Dutch word meaning "open grassland" or "field," symbolizes vast opportunities and untapped potential. It’s no longer just about cleaning spaces—it’s about **cleaning up the system**, breaking down barriers, creating job opportunities, and making services more accessible through technology.

And this is just the beginning. The vision for Veldt extends far beyond an app. The long-term goal is to build **The Veldt Group of Companies**—a network of businesses dedicated to driving innovation, empowering communities, and transforming Namibia’s economic landscape.

We’re not just another app. **We’re a movement.** And this is only the start.

Our Mission

To **redefine service delivery in Namibia** by creating a **seamless digital platform** where skilled professionals and clients connect effortlessly. Veldt is more than just a marketplace—it’s a movement towards **economic empowerment, technological advancement, and greater accessibility** for all Namibians.

Our Vision

To make **Veldt the most trusted and accessible service platform in Namibia**, setting new standards for **quality, reliability, and efficiency** in professional services. Our goal is to drive **digital transformation across multiple industries**, ensuring that every Namibian can easily access the services they need, whenever they need them.

Our Values

At the core of Veldt are **four guiding values**:

  • Trust: We foster a **secure and reliable** environment where clients and service providers can connect with confidence.
  • Excellence: We maintain **high standards** in service quality, ensuring an outstanding experience for all users.
  • Innovation: We embrace **technology and forward-thinking solutions** to improve efficiency and user experience.
  • Community: We are committed to **uplifting and empowering** Namibians by creating economic opportunities and driving growth.

The Future: Veldt Group of Companies

Veldt began as a **service booking app**, but its purpose extends far beyond that. This is just the beginning.

The long-term vision is to build the **Veldt Group of Companies**, an ecosystem of businesses dedicated to **technology, innovation, and economic development** in Namibia. Each company within the group will tackle different challenges—from **digital transformation to job creation**—while sharing a single goal: to **empower Namibians and reshape industries for the better**.

We are not just **building an app**; we are **building a future** where every Namibian has access to high-quality services, greater opportunities, and a thriving economy.
