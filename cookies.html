Opaleka Cookies Policy

Learn how we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help improve website functionality, enhance security, and provide analytics.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality, such as user authentication and security.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze website traffic and improve the user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and personalize your experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and measure advertising effectiveness.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control cookie settings in your browser and delete cookies at any time. However, disabling certain cookies may impact your experience on our website.
